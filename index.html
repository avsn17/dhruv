our journey

chapter 1

Introduction to Prelog

It began with something he said through everything that followed: "Stay Fearless". It was what remained. I didn't know what awaited us, but somewhere I heard and I knew, without fully understanding why, that I needed to introduce myself to him.

chapter 2

If commitment is time...

Our words were foreplay to what was yet to come. Then I needed him to understand something essential: Distance made us who we were; gaps in between words creating paragraphs to texts, and whatever seemed challenging to others was essentially to us.

chapter 3

Growing towards Each Other

There's a moment when you stop protecting yourself. When you offer up the parts of you that you've spent years cultivating cautiously. I reached that moment with him and offered him my trust. A puzzle piece of mine fitting nearly perfectly into his.

chapter 4

2 for 1

Intimacy doesn't arrive in declarations. It arrives when they see you and don't turn away.

chapter 5

Breathing into dive deep

A moment arrives when you realize there's no going back when the safety of the surface no longer appeals to you. When you're willing to dive into the unknown because staying shallow is no longer an option.

chapter 6

Who Do You See?

The questions that matter most - the ones we answer through observation, through attention, through the choice to truly look at another person. I looked and I liked what I saw.

chapter 7

Burning Bright

And then I understood what we were. Something where two people recognize in each other a similar heat, making them move closer creating something of and for themselves.
